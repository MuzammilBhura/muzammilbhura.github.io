🔒 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 8, 2026

1. Who We Are

Medobo is a medical knowledge application designed for healthcare professionals, students, and general users who want to understand how medicine works. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google.

Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the app — features accessed, content viewed, and quiz or clinical case activity. This helps us improve the app experience.

Device Information

We collect basic device information including device type, operating system version, and app version for bug reporting and support purposes.

Feedback and Reports

If you submit feedback or report an issue, we collect the content of your message along with your account details and device information.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain your account
  • To personalise your app experience
  • To process subscription and premium access
  • To send notifications about new content, updates, and challenges (you can opt out at any time)
  • To analyse usage patterns and improve content quality
  • To respond to feedback and support requests

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Advertising

Free tier users may see advertisements served through third-party ad networks. These networks may collect anonymised data to show relevant ads. We do not pass personally identifiable information to advertisers.

6. Medical Disclaimer

All content in Medobo is for educational purposes only. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. We are not responsible for clinical decisions made based on content in this app.

7. Children

Medobo is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You may request to view, update, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting us. You can delete your account from within the app settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of Medobo after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us through the feedback option in the app or at medoboapp@gmail.com.
